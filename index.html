Are you ready to achieve all your dreams, tour the world rocking out for your adoring fans, and roll around in piles of cash!? All you need is instruments for yourself and your four bandmates, and $1,000.00 for recording your first big hit!
But wait! You've got an up-and-coming rival trying to top the charts before you! Whoever gathers all the necessary goods first wins the glory and the record deal!

To play this game, you must match 3 of each item to acquire it in your inventory. You may click any item and its neighbor to make them switch places, in order to cause matches. You cannot click the 'lazy bandmate' or the 'demanding boss', because they just get in the way! Welcome to the life of a musician. After player one has won the game, his or her time will be logged on the right side of the page, and player 2 will have a chance to beat player one's time. Good luck!
